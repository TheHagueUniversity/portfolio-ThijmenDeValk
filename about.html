Thijmen de Valk

Hi there! I'm a Dutch product designer.

I love designing products that blend the digital and physical world, and I will admit I like my creations to be seen and used by people.

I started coding when I was 8, and ever since I was 14 I wanted to be a filmmaker. Then I tried my hand at some marketing and even (oh snap) acting. For some reason (that is way too long to explain here), that perfectly leveled out in product design. And the rest is history, as they say.

Currently, I'm studying at THUAS in The Hague. I'm also still freelancing, because... well, because I am.
